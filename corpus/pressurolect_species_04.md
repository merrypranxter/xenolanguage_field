# Pressurolect: A Language for Harmonic Resonance Intelligences

## Signal Units

The basic unit is a **resonant event** — a mechanically sustained or transient pressure wave:
- **Fundamental frequency**: determines temporal and semantic register (low = ancient/universal; high = immediate/urgent)
- **Harmonic series**: integer overtones above the fundamental specify, qualify, and emotionally texture the base meaning
- **Amplitude envelope**: sustained = committed/permanent; transient = observational/exploratory
- **Phase relationship**: relative phase to the speaker's own signature encodes grammatical role
- **Beat frequency**: two close-but-unequal frequencies create a beating oscillation encoding ambiguity, tension, or genuine complexity that cannot be resolved into a single frequency

No phonemes. No discrete symbols. Just physics.

## Combinatorics

### Harmonic Stack (AND / COMPOUND MEANING)
Adding integer overtones to a base frequency creates compound, specified meaning. A bare fundamental is a category. Overtones are specification.

Example: `[f₀: social-frequency] + [2f₀: specific-individual-resonance] + [3f₀: extended-contact]` = "extended greeting to a known individual"

### Beat Frequency (TENSION / AMBIGUITY)
Two frequencies close enough to produce a beat: the difference frequency oscillates as a slow modulation. Encodes emotional complexity, irresolution, or honest ambiguity.

Example: `[f_safety + ε] ⊗ [f_safety]` = "I feel safe but I have reservations I cannot yet isolate"

### Resonance Lock (≡ SHARED RESONANCE)
Two singers vibrating at identical frequency, in phase. Constructive interference. The shared standing wave amplitude exceeds either singer's individual signal. This is the most intimate register: the singers are no longer communicating with each other — they are together in a single physical state.

Example: `[f_A] → [f_A] ≡ [f_B] → [f_B]` = resonance lock converged from two initial frequencies

### Damping (END / RELEASE / TERMINAL CADENCE)
Deliberate, structured amplitude reduction. The profile of the decay encodes the type of ending. Rapid = termination. Exponential = natural completion. Oscillating fade = unresolved.

### Fundamental Shift (BECOME / JOURNEY / CHANGE)
Sliding the fundamental frequency. Upward = toward urgency/precision. Downward = toward depth/history/universality.
