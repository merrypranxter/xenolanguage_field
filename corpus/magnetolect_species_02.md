# Magnetolect: A Language for Field-Distributed Intelligences

## Signal Units

The basic unit is a **field perturbation** — a localized distortion in the ambient magnetic topology:
- **Polarity**: North-seeking or South-seeking flux concentration
- **Gradient**: How sharply the field changes (analogous to "volume")
- **Topology**: Which field line the perturbation rides; encodes "identity" and "address"
- **Duration**: How long the perturbation persists; encodes "tense" and "commitment"

No sounds. No gestures. Just field.

## Combinatorics

### Superposition (AND)
Two perturbations on the same field line interfere. The resultant field encodes both meanings.
The receiver senses the combined topology directly.

### Bifurcation (OR)
A perturbation splits across multiple field lines. This creates parallel meaning — 
the equivalent of "on one hand... on the other hand..."

### Reconnection (BECAUSE)
Two field lines that were separate merge. This encodes causation: the bringing-together of previously unrelated concepts.

### Pinch (URGENT)
A field line pinches off, forming a localized island. This is a command, a name, or a spell — 
something that must be remembered exactly because the topology is now isolated.
